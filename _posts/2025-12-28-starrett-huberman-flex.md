Dr. Andrew Huberman and Dr. Kelly Starrett discuss the importance of integrating daily floor sitting into your routine, explaining how it preserves natural movement ranges, improves joint health, and reduces risks of aging-related conditions like hip and back pain.

Dr. Kelly Starrett is a world-renowned physical therapist, best-selling author, and expert on improving movement in fitness, sports, and daily life. Dr. Andrew Huberman is a tenured professor of neurobiology and ophthalmology at Stanford University School of Medicine and host of the Huberman Lab podcast.
